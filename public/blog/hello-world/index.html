Hello, World

21.05.2017 19:43

Hi! Welcome to my latest (and hopefully greatest) website.

I’ve never been very good at maintaining a website/blog, but it’s 2017 and I really ought to have one, so here it is.

After several attempts over the years on various different platforms (Blogger, Wordpress, and even a brief version hosted on GitHub Pages), I’ve finally settled on a static site powered by Hugo and self-hosted (a VPS from OVH at the time of writing).

Over the next few weeks I’ll be posting a few various tips and tricks I’ve found over the years, and I’ll also be documenting my slow decent into madness as I attempt to setup a Windows gaming VM on a Linux ghost using PCI passthrough - wish me luck.
